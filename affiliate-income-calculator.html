What is affiliate income?

Affiliate income is money earned when you recommend a product or service and receive a commission after someone buys through your link.

Formula used

Sales = Monthly Visitors × Conversion Rate

Net Income = Sales × Commission Per Sale − Refunds

Example

If 1,000 people visit your page, 2% buy, and you earn $40 per sale, your gross commission is $800 before refunds.
